Feel Good Fund

The Redgate Feel Good Fund is dedicated to providing fun stuff like team days out. Our Cambridge office has held cheese and wine tastings, been on punt trips down the River Cam, and has an annual black tie Christmas Ball. Our Austin office has gone indoor skydiving at iFLY, attends local happy hours and trivia nights, and has an annual holiday party.
